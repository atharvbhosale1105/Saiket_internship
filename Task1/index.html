Projects

Student Recruitment System

The Student Recruitment System is a web-based application designed to streamline the process of student assessment and recruitment. The system allows students to register, create and update their profiles, and participate in multiple evaluation stages such as Test 1, Test 2, and interviews. Progression to the next stage depends on performance in the previous stage.

Samrt Campus Complaint Management and Booking System

The Smart Campus Complaint Management and Booking System is a centralized platform developed to enhance campus facility management. It allows students and staff to register complaints related to campus infrastructure such as classrooms, hostels, electrical issues, or sanitation, and track their resolution status in real time.

Crop Monitoring System

The Crop Monitoring System is a technology-driven solution designed to assist farmers and agricultural organizations in monitoring crop health and environmental conditions. The system collects data such as soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and crop growth status using sensors or manual inputs.
